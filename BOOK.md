# The Book of Tindex

> *An institution preserves its future by preserving its knowledge.*

---

# Welcome

Welcome to **The Book of Tindex**.

This repository is not documentation.

It is not a software manual.

It is not a specification repository.

It is the institutional memory of Tindex.

Its purpose is to preserve the knowledge, reasoning, engineering practices, decisions, and history required to successfully complete, operate, and evolve Tindex over time.

Nothing important about Tindex should exist only in conversations.

If knowledge is important enough to influence the future of the project, it belongs in this repository.

---

# Purpose

The Book exists to ensure that Tindex can continue successfully regardless of:

* time,
* technology,
* contributors,
* AI assistants,
* or individual conversations.

The Book preserves not only **what** was decided, but **why** it was decided.

---

# Guiding Principle

> **Reduce uncertainty through evidence.**

This principle guides every engineering decision, architectural review, and institutional process within Tindex.

---

# Repository Structure

The Book is organized into several major sections.

## Constitution

Stable principles that rarely change.

Examples include:

* Manifesto
* North Star
* Mission
* Vision
* Core Principles

---

## Living Knowledge

Continuously updated operational knowledge.

Examples include:

* Project Status
* Roadmap
* Milestones
* Engineering Log

---

## Historical Record

The reasoning behind major decisions.

This includes:

* architectural evolution,
* rejected alternatives,
* lessons learned,
* institutional history.

---

## Continuation

Guidance for future contributors and future AI-assisted engineering sessions.

The goal is continuity without unnecessary rediscovery.

---

# Relationship to Other Repositories

The Tindex ecosystem is intentionally separated into repositories with distinct responsibilities.

## tindex-book

Institutional memory.

## tindex-specifications

Canonical truth.

## tindex-core

Software implementation.

## tindex-platform

Infrastructure and operations.

Each repository serves a different purpose.

Together they form the institutional architecture of Tindex.
